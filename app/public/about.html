What is Four Minute Drill?

Four Minute Drill is a free daily NFL football game. Each day you draft six real players — quarterback, running back, two receivers, tight end, kicker — under a $25 salary cap, then call every play of one do-or-die drive: 4:00 on the clock, down 3, ball on your own 20.

Everyone in the world gets the same daily board and the same drive. Only your calls differ — which makes every score comparable, and every group chat an argument.

Real football, not dice

Every outcome is sampled from that player's actual NFL play-by-play history (2015–2025, via nflverse) in similar situations — down, distance, field position. Drafting a shaky kicker is exactly as terrifying as it should be.

Scoring

Touchdown = 100 · Field goal = 40, multiplied by a clutch bonus — the less clock left when you score, the bigger (up to ×2 at 0:00). Come up short and you still bank half a point per yard your drive advanced.

Ghost racing

Share links carry your drive's exact play-by-play. A friend who opens one doesn't just see your score — they race your actual drive, clock-synced, on the same field. Beat a ghost, become the ghost.

The details

New daily at midnight ET · one shot per daily, unlimited free play · no account, no install, ~90 seconds · leaderboards for today, all-time, and win streaks · privacy (short version: we store almost nothing).
