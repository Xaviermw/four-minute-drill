# Launch kit — September 2026

Draft materials for kickoff-week launch. Everything here is copy to adapt, not
scripture. The one rule: every pitch leads with the **shared-deal fact**
("everyone gets the same game") or the **ghost race**, never with features.

## Positioning (one breath)

> Four Minute Drill is a free daily football game: build a team under a $25
> cap, then call every play of one do-or-die drive — 4:00 left, down 3.
> Everyone in the world gets the same board and the same drive. Only your
> calls differ. Takes about 90 seconds.

Secondary hook (for football/fantasy audiences): **ghost racing** — share your
result and your friend doesn't just see your score, they *race your actual
drive*, play for play, on the same field.

## Reddit (r/fantasyfootball, r/nfl — read each sub's self-promo rules first;
participate for a week before posting; Saturday mornings do well)

**Title options:**
- "I built a free daily game where everyone gets the same 2-minute drill —
  same players, same cap, same drive. Only your play calls differ."
- "Draft 6 players under a $25 cap, then call every play of one do-or-die
  drive. Made this as a daily — same board for everyone."

**Body (short — Reddit hates walls):**
> Rules: $25 cap, six slots, three options each (scrubs are always $0 if you
> go broke). Then it's 4:00 left, down 3, own 20 — you call everything,
> including tempo. Outcomes come from each player's real play-by-play history
> (nflverse), so drafting a bad kicker is exactly as terrifying as it should
> be. One shared daily per day; losses still count for yards. Free, no
> account, ~90 seconds. Would love feedback on the pricing — arguing about
> whether a guy is worth $4 is half the game.

(That last line is deliberate: price debates ARE the comment section.)

## Podcast / newsletter one-pager (fantasy shows, "same daily" segment)

**The pitch (email, 6 sentences):**
> Subject: A 90-second segment your listeners can play along with
>
> Four Minute Drill is a free daily football game — everyone gets the same
> $25-cap draft and the same do-or-die drive. Because the whole day is one
> shared deal, it works as a recurring segment: you and your co-hosts play the
> same daily before the show and compare scores on air; listeners play the
> same board and post theirs. Every result makes a share link that lets
> anyone race your exact drive play-by-play — so hosts can literally say
> "beat my drive" and drop one link. No account, no app install, ~90 seconds.
> Happy to make you a custom theme day (your show's name on a $15 cap day) if
> it lands. [link]

**Custom theme days as outreach currency**: the cap scaffold supports one-line
specials — offering "The [Show] $15 Challenge Day" costs us a config entry and
gives a podcast a reason to send their audience on a specific date.

## Timing & checklist

- **Don't launch before the data refresh** (2026 rosters, August task).
- Freeze: no scoring/economy changes after Sept 1.
- Pre-launch gates: D7 ≥ 15% on the soft-launch cohort; privacy page live;
  domain live; Sentry on; boards seeded modestly.
- Launch order: PH (Tue/Wed of kickoff week) → Reddit that weekend →
  podcast emails the week after (they book ahead — send those FIRST, in
  August, for September slots).
